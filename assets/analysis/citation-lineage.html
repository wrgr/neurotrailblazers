What is Citation Ancestry?
This visualization shows how knowledge builds in connectomics research. Papers at the bottom are foundational works (cited by many others), while papers at the top are more recent (cite foundational work). The lines show direct citation relationships. Click a paper to highlight its citations; hover to see full details. This is a real citation network, not just milestone papers.

How to Read This

• Vertical position (generation): Shows knowledge depth. Bottom = foundational works, Top = recent work.
• Circle size: Larger circles = more highly cited papers (more impact).
• Line color: Darker = stronger citation relationships.
• Generations: Gen 0 papers don't cite others in corpus; Gen 1 cite Gen 0; Gen 2 cite Gen 0-1.
• Interactive: Click any paper to highlight its citations (incoming and outgoing).
