# Deck Spec: 02 Brain Data Across Scales

## Session
- Duration: 75 + 15 min
- Outcome: scale-selection worksheet
- Figure source: `course/units/figures/02-brain-data-across-scales-selected-v1.md`

## Slide plan
1. Title and learning objective
   - Content: scale as a scientific validity choice.
2. Scale ladder overview
   - Content: mesoscale to ultrastructure and resolvable features.
3. Acquisition vs analysis scale
   - Content: mismatch pitfalls.
4. Representation stack
   - Content: volume -> segmentation -> skeleton/mesh -> graph.
5. Registration framework
   - Content: rigid/affine/non-linear transforms.
6. Uncertainty propagation
   - Content: residuals and confidence reporting.
7. Anisotropy risks
   - Content: axial under-resolution artifacts.
8. Compute and storage implications
   - Content: chunking, I/O, query latency by scale.
9. Worked comparison
   - Content: same question at two scales, different conclusions.
10. Failure modes
    - Content: scale leakage, over-registration confidence.
11. Decision template
    - Content: minimum sufficient scale protocol.
12. Activity + bridge
    - Content: defend one scale choice; bridge to EM acquisition.

## Assessment
- Prompt: choose one research question and justify scale + transform + representation choices.
- Rubric: resolvability, traceability, resource realism.
