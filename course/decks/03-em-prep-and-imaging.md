# Deck Spec: 03 EM Prep and Imaging

## Session
- Duration: 75 + 15 min
- Outcome: acquisition risk register and QA gate list
- Figure source: `course/units/figures/03-em-prep-and-imaging-selected-v1.md`

## Slide plan
1. Title and scope
   - Content: acquisition quality as upper bound.
2. End-to-end acquisition chain
   - Content: fixation -> staining -> sectioning -> imaging.
3. Imaging parameter tradeoffs
   - Content: dwell, current, overlap, throughput.
4. Artifact classes I
   - Content: folds, tears, chatter, compression.
5. Artifact classes II
   - Content: charging, contrast drift, misalignment.
6. Downstream consequences
   - Content: merge/split and continuity errors.
7. QA metrics
   - Content: SNR, seam rate, residual alignment error.
8. Pilot segmentation gate
   - Content: before full-volume processing.
9. Metadata and provenance
   - Content: required acquisition logs.
10. Failure escalation
    - Content: stop/go triggers and remediation paths.
11. Worked triage case
    - Content: identify artifact, impact, and mitigation.
12. Activity + bridge
    - Content: create risk register; bridge to infrastructure.

## Assessment
- Prompt: list three artifact risks with detection metric and mitigation action.
- Rubric: technical specificity, feasibility, downstream awareness.
