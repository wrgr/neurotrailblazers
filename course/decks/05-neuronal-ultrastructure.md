# Deck Spec: 05 Neuronal Ultrastructure

## Session
- Duration: 75 + 20 min
- Outcome: cue-based annotation sheet with confidence labels
- Figure source: `course/units/figures/05-neuronal-ultrastructure-selected-v1.md`

## Slide plan
1. Title and practical objective
   - Content: ultrastructure as annotation language.
2. Compartment refresher
   - Content: soma, dendrite, axon, spine, bouton.
3. Synaptic cues
   - Content: vesicles, active zone, PSD, cleft context.
4. Organelle cues
   - Content: mitochondria, ER, microtubule patterns.
5. Multi-slice protocol
   - Content: avoid single-slice calls.
6. Evidence triangulation
   - Content: morphology + organelles + neighborhood context.
7. Worked call I
   - Content: straightforward label with high confidence.
8. Worked call II
   - Content: ambiguity and provisional labeling.
9. Common failure modes
   - Content: noisy contrast and false synapse positives.
10. QC metrics
    - Content: inter-rater agreement and uncertainty rates.
11. Decision-log format
    - Content: reproducible annotation records.
12. Activity + bridge
    - Content: label three cues and confidence; bridge to axon/dendrite discrimination.

## Assessment
- Prompt: annotate one panel and justify decisions with three independent cues.
- Rubric: cue quality, coherence, uncertainty handling.
