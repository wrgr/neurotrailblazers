# 01 Why Map the Brain
Technical Training: Nanoscale Connectomics

---

## Core framing
- Connectomics translates broad neuroscience goals into measurable structural questions.
- Structure constrains candidate computations; it does not alone establish causal function.

---

## The motivating question
Can wiring structure provide reusable constraints for neuroscience and AI hypotheses?

---

## What structure can support
- Motif frequency hypotheses.
- Cell-type-specific targeting tests.
- Connectivity-derived candidate mechanisms.

---

## What structure cannot prove alone
- Dynamic state trajectories.
- Real-time causal dependence.
- Full behavioral mechanism without complementary evidence.

---

## Hypothesis translation workflow
Biological question -> measurable structural endpoint -> null model -> interpretation boundary.

---

## Evidence requirements
- Define reconstruction completeness target.
- Define expected annotation quality thresholds.
- Pre-state uncertainty handling rules.

---

## Failure modes
- Claim inflation.
- Metric mismatch.
- Dataset mismatch to question scale.

---

## Activity
Draft one hypothesis brief with:
- metric,
- null model,
- one explicit non-claim.
