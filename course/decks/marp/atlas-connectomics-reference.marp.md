# Atlas Connectomics Reference
Technical Training: Nanoscale Connectomics

---

## Purpose
Operational reference system for papers, datasets, and tools tied to the technical track.

---

## Required metadata
- citation
- workflow stage
- modality + resolution
- access level
- maturity
- known limits
- mapped unit(s)

---

## Curation principles
- Prefer resources with reproducible artifacts.
- Keep historical resources labeled as historical.
- Remove stale links and duplicated entries.

---

## Workflow-stage indexing
Acquisition | Reconstruction | Proofreading | Analysis | Cross-cutting

---

## Quality checks
- Link health.
- Metadata completeness.
- Stage coverage balance.
- Update timestamp and owner.

---

## Failure modes
- Bibliography without operational metadata.
- Overweighting one stage.
- Missing limitation statements.

---

## Activity
Curate one entry with complete metadata and one explicit limitation.

---

## Wrap
Use atlas updates to drive journal-club and module refresh cycles.
