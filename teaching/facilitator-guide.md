  <section class="section">
    <h2>Delivery Model</h2>
    <p>Each module is designed for a 60-minute instructional core with optional extension work. Use the same lesson flow in every session:</p>
    <ol>
      <li><strong>Learn:</strong> capability target + concept framing</li>
      <li><strong>Practice:</strong> studio activity with evidence-based outputs</li>
      <li><strong>Check:</strong> rubric-based competency check</li>
      <li><strong>Teach:</strong> slide deck + worksheet for transfer and reuse</li>
    </ol>
  </section>

  <section class="section">
    <h2>Recommended Session Template (60 minutes)</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>00:00-08:00 - Goal framing and prior-knowledge activation</li>
      <li>08:00-20:00 - Instructor modeling of one worked example</li>
      <li>20:00-38:00 - Guided learner activity</li>
      <li>38:00-50:00 - Debrief and misconception correction</li>
      <li>50:00-58:00 - Competency check</li>
      <li>58:00-60:00 - Exit prompt and next-step assignment</li>
    </ol>
  </section>

  <section class="section">
    <h2>Quality Checks for Facilitators</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Every learner output includes evidence, interpretation, and limitation.</li>
      <li>Uncertainty language is normalized and rewarded, not penalized.</li>
      <li>Hidden-curriculum norms are stated explicitly (expectations, escalation paths, communication etiquette).</li>
      <li>Rubric feedback is specific and linked to observable behaviors.</li>
    </ul>
  </section>
